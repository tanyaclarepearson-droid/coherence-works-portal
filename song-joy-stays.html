Joy
Stays

Joy as a condition for learning, memory, belonging, and return.

Joy Stays carries one of the ecosystem’s most important messages: joy is not extra, decorative, or earned after the work. Joy is one of the conditions that helps learning become memorable, relational, and safe enough to stay.

About this song
☀

Developmental Function

Reframes joy as a learning condition rather than a break from learning.

♡

What It Reinforces

Safety, openness, wonder, belonging, and the emotional conditions that help memory hold.

↻

Classroom Use

Use before a lesson, during community-building, or when the room needs warmth before stretch.

Core message: Joy isn’t a reward. It’s the door.
