Tiny Classroom Examples

Coherence in practice.

Small moves that make conditions visible, repeatable, and easier to sustain.

View Calmer Transitions Journey →Next: Reflection ToolExplore Snapshot Gallery

Use these as entry points

No full program required. Pick one small shift and observe what changes.

Examples

Transition reset

Play one familiar song, breathe once, and give a clear next-step sentence.

Participation lift

Add one choice: partner, tool, sentence frame, or role.

Regulation support

Use a bell, bowl, nature image, or quiet visual cue before independent work.

3F check

Ask: Is the form clear? Is the rhythm predictable? Is the routine consistent?

CREA moment

Notice when a condition creates resonance and a student begins to lead.

DPZ seed

Repeat what works until students can carry it with less adult input.
