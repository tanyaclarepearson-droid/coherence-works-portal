Example Journey:
Calmer Transitions

A full pathway from “What if…” to reflection.

This sample shows how one small classroom move can travel through Teacher Mode, 3F, CREA, and the reflection tool.

Back to What If Paths → Open Reflection Tool

What if...

What if transitions felt calmer and easier to return from?

This is a practical entry point for any teacher who wants less friction and more readiness between learning moments.

The Journey

1. Teacher Mode

  • Notice: Energy rises after partner work.
  • Tune: Add a predictable return cue.
  • Intend: Help students re-enter learning with less friction.

2. 3F

  • Form: Clear transition routine.
  • Frequency: Repeat daily after group work.
  • Fidelity: Same cue, same tone, same next step.

3. CREA

  • Conditions: Song, bell, breath, or visual cue.
  • Resonance: Students recognize the pattern.
  • Emergence: Students begin settling sooner.
  • Agency: Students start preparing before the cue ends.

4. Classroom Move

Play one familiar 30–60 second audio cue, breathe once, and name the next step.

Teacher script: “When the cue ends, materials are still, bodies are settled, and eyes are ready for the next step.”

Reflection Endpoint

What changed?

What changed in the room after the cue?

What stabilized?

Did students recover attention more quickly or with less adult prompting?

What repeats?

What should happen again tomorrow with the same form, frequency, and fidelity?
